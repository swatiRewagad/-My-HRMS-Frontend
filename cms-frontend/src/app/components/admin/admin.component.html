CMS Back Office

Internal modules for complaint processing, categorization, and administration

Email Simulation (Demo)

Simulate email-based complaint filing. Incoming emails are auto-parsed, categorized, and a structured form is sent back to the complainant.

  • Inbound email parsing
  • Auto-categorization via business rules
  • Auto-reply with complaint form
  • Thread tracking

Physical Complaint (AI Assisted)

Process scanned handwritten/printed complaint letters using Gemini OCR. AI extracts text, identifies fields, suggests categories, and provides resolution recommendations.

  • Gemini AI-powered OCR
  • Hindi + English handwriting support
  • Auto field extraction
  • AI suggestions from past resolutions

Business Rules - Auto Categorization

Configure keyword-based rules to automatically categorize incoming complaints from any source (email, physical, online). Rules determine category and priority assignment.

  • Keyword-based rule engine
  • Source-specific rules (email/physical/online)
  • Priority auto-assignment
  • Test panel to validate rules
